MyWords
Smart Dictionary & Vocabulary Builder
MyWords is more than just an English-English and English-Vietnamese dictionary. It helps you create your own word collection, memorize vocabulary with Spaced Repetition, and practice through fun quizzes that make learning more engaging and effective.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my personal word collection?
To build your personal word collection, simply search for any word in the dictionary and tap the "Add to Collection" button. You can organize your words into custom categories and add personal notes to help with memorization.
What is Spaced Repetition and how does it work?
Spaced Repetition is a scientifically-proven learning technique that shows you words at increasing intervals. Words you find difficult appear more frequently, while words you know well appear less often. This optimizes your learning efficiency and long-term retention.
Can I use MyWords offline?
Yes! MyWords includes an offline dictionary feature that allows you to look up words and review your personal collection even without an internet connection. Some advanced features may require connectivity.
How do the quizzes help with vocabulary learning?
Our interactive quizzes test your knowledge through multiple formats: multiple choice, fill-in-the-blank, and pronunciation challenges. The quiz system adapts to your performance, focusing on words that need more practice.
Is there a limit to how many words I can save?
There's no limit to your personal word collection! Save as many words as you'd like and organize them however works best for your learning style.
Can I sync my progress across multiple devices?
Yes, create an account to sync your word collections, learning progress, and quiz results across all your devices. Your vocabulary journey continues seamlessly wherever you are.

Troubleshooting

The app is running slowly or crashing
Try restarting the app and ensuring you have the latest version installed. Clear the app cache if the problem persists. If you have a large word collection, the app may need a moment to load all your data.
I can't hear pronunciation audio
Check your device's volume settings and ensure the app has permission to play audio. Some pronunciations require an internet connection to download.
My word collection disappeared
If you have an account, try signing out and back in to sync your data. Contact support if you're still missing words - we may be able to help recover your collection.
